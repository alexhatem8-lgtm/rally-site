Support

Questions, problems, feedback, or reports — we read everything and respond fast.

Contact us

Email support@rallyfindyourcrowd.com and we'll get back to you, usually within 24 hours.

Reporting content or users

The fastest path is in the app: every profile, post, message, event, tailgate, and watch party has a report option in its menu, and you can block any user from their profile. Reports are reviewed and acted on, typically within 24 hours.

Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account any time in the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This removes your personal information as described in our Privacy Policy.
