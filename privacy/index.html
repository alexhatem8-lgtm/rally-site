Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Rally handles your information. Rally helps sports fans discover games, join tailgates and watch parties, and chat with other fans.

Information we collect

Account info (your email, and a password if you set one). Profile info you choose to add (display name, username, bio, home city, photo, favorite teams, fan tags). Location while you use the app, with your permission, to show nearby events on the map — we do not track location in the background. Content you create (posts, comments, chat messages, photos, gatherings, listings). Usage and device info, including a push notification token. Your phone number only if you choose to verify it.

How we use your information

To run your account and profile, show relevant nearby events and recommendations, enable social features (friends, groups, chat, posts, gatherings), send notifications you have enabled, keep Rally safe (enforce our Terms, review reports, moderate content), and respond to support requests.

How information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. Your public profile and content are visible to other users per the app’s features. We use service providers to run the app: chat (Stream), file/photo storage (Cloudflare R2), email (Resend), hosting and database (Render), sports data (TheSportsDB), maps/geocoding (the device map provider and OpenStreetMap), and push delivery (Apple/Expo). We may share information for legal reasons or to protect Rally and its users.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your information while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account any time in Settings → Delete Account; we then remove or anonymize your personal information. Some records may be retained where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Your choices

Turn location and notifications on or off in your device settings. Edit or remove profile details in the app. Delete your account in-app, or email us with questions about your data. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights to access or correct your data.

Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted connections, hashed passwords, access controls) to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

Children

Rally is not intended for anyone under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy and will post the new version with an updated date. Questions? Email support@rallyfindyourcrowd.com.
