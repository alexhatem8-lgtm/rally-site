Find your crowd on game day.

Rally is the social app for sports fans — every game becomes a place to meet up, talk trash, tailgate, and watch together.

Download on the App Store Free · iPhone · Ages 18+

What Rally does

Pick your teams and Rally builds your game day around them — the games, the people, and the plans, all in one app.

💬

Game-day chats

Every game has a live room. Jump in before kickoff, celebrate (or commiserate) with fans of your team — no invite needed.

🔥

Tailgates & watch parties

Host or join real meetups around any game — lot parties before kickoff, bar takeovers for away games, living-room watch parties. Public or invite-only.

🤝

Meet fans like you

A swipe deck of fans near you who love the same teams. Say hey — if it’s mutual, you’re friends with a chat ready to plan game day.

🎟️

Ticket marketplace

Buy and sell tickets fan-to-fan. Tickets are held until the buyer confirms delivery, and sellers are paid out securely through Stripe.

Your team plays better with a crowd.
